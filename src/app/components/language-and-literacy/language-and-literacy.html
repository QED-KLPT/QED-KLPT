Language and Literacy

The language and literacy progression describes children’s ability to communicate using spoken word, sign language, or AAC.   This section supports you to observe and reflect on a child’s understanding of language and ability to express themselves effectively. The focus of this progression is on communication skills and literacy-related language, including understanding written texts and narrative structures.
