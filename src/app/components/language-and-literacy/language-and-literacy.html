Language and literacy

Language and literacy learning in the KLPT focuses on communication skills and literacy related language including age and developmentally appropriate exposure to text and stories. These skills develop over time and are shaped by who children are, their identity, culture, the language they speak at home, and their daily experiences.

Overview

The Language and literacy progression focuses on how children use words through speech, sign, or Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) in order to share their thoughts and understand what others say, sign or write.
