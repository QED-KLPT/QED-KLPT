Learning domain

Social-emotional learning

Social learning involves children learning in pairs, small groups or as a whole group. Collaborative learning opportunities in early childhood help the development of children’s communication skills, social skills, social regulation skills and awareness of others.

Emotional learning begins in infancy and continues into adolescence. Children learn to identify and regulate their emotions and understand the emotions of others.
