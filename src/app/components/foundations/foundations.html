KLPT foundations

The KLPT helps teachers and educators use high-quality assessment practices to build children’s capabilities across five learning domains. It supports decision-making and planning and helps teachers and educators gather evidence of what children know, can do and understand. The KLPT recognises and values children’s diverse ways of knowing, being and doing, including the many ways children express themselves, connect with others and make meaning.

The strength-based KLPT acknowledges Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander worldviews, and centres the importance of culture, traditions, family and kinship in a child’s learning. Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander perspectives is intentionally embedded throughout the toolkit.
