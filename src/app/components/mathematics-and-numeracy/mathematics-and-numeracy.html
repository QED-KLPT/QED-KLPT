Mathematics and numeracy

The mathematics and numeracy progressions relate to children’s ability to understand, describe, discuss, and think about their environment. This progression describes the development of numbers for counting and understanding quantity. It supports the use of rich language to talk and think about concepts of time, length, distance, capacity, and area in everyday activities.
