Testing Footer

The following demos showcase the app-footer-shared component with various configurations and variants inspired by the Queensland Government Design System.

1. Default Footer

The standard Queensland Government footer with all sections: contact information, quick links, social media links, and acknowledgement of traditional owners.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Department of Education"
  [contactText]="Get in touch for enquiries..."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  [linkColumns]="defaultLinks"
  [socialLinks]="defaultSocialLinksArr"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."
  copyrightText="© The State of Queensland (Department of Education) 2026"
  qgovLink="Queensland Government"
  [qgovHref]="'https://www.qld.gov.au/'"></app-footer-shared>

2. Dark Footer

A dark background variant using the 'dark' theme. Suitable for applications that need a deeper, more formal footer appearance.

<app-footer-shared
  variant="dark"
  title="Department of Education"
  [contactText]="Get in touch for enquiries..."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  [linkColumns]="defaultLinks"
  [socialLinks]="defaultSocialLinksArr"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."></app-footer-shared>

3. Minimal Footer

A simplified footer without optional link columns and social media. Best for internal tools or applications that need a lightweight footer.

<app-footer-shared
  variant="minimal"
  title="Your Application"
  [contactText]="Need help? Get in touch."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  [linkColumns]="minimalLinks"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."></app-footer-shared>

4. Contact Focused Footer

A footer that emphasizes contact information with a prominent phone number and contact button. Ideal for service-oriented applications.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Child Services"
  contactText="Get in touch for enquiries..."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  contactButtonText="Contact us"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."></app-footer-shared>

5. Multi-Column Links Footer

A footer with multiple link columns for organizing many navigation options. Best for large portals or government websites.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Queensland Government Portal"
  [contactText]="Get in touch for enquiries..."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  [linkColumns]="multiLinks"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."></app-footer-shared>

6. Social Media Footer

A footer with prominent social media links for community engagement. Best for public-facing applications that want to encourage social interaction.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Community Connect"

7. Acknowledgement Only Footer

A minimal footer showing only the title and acknowledgement of traditional owners. Best for simple applications or pages that need legal compliance without extra content.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Learning Portal"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges..."
  copyrightText="© The State of Queensland 2026"
  qgovLink="Queensland Government"></app-footer-shared>

8. Simple Two-Column Footer

A compact footer with just the title and navigation links. No social media, contact details, or acknowledgement. Best for internal tools and dashboards.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Internal Dashboard"
  contactText="For support, contact the IT helpdesk."
  phoneNumber="07 1234 5678"
  contactButtonText="Get Help"
  [linkColumns]="simpleLinks"></app-footer-shared>

10. Government Standard Footer

The complete Queensland Government standard footer with all required sections: contact, legal links, social media, acknowledgement, and copyright. This is the recommended configuration for all Queensland Government applications.

<app-footer-shared
  title="Department of Education"
  contactText="Get in touch for enquiries, feedback..."
  phoneNumber="13 QGOV (13 74 68)"
  contactButtonText="Contact us"
  [linkColumns]="governmentLinks"
  [socialLinks]="defaultSocialLinksArr"
  acknowledgementText="The Department of Education acknowledges the Traditional Owners..."
  copyrightText="© The State of Queensland (Department of Education) 2026"
  qgovLink="Queensland Government"></app-footer-shared>
