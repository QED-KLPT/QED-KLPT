Testing Cards

The following demos showcase the app-card component with various types, variants, and configurations following the Queensland Government Design System.

1. No Action Card

A basic non-clickable card with just a title and description. Used for informational content that doesn't require user interaction.

<app-card title="No Action Card"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="no-action"></app-card>

2. Single Action Default

A clickable card with a link in the title. The entire card area is interactive and shows a hover state.

<app-card title="Single Action Card"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="single-action"
  href="#"></app-card>

3. Single Action with Arrow

A single action card with a trailing arrow icon after the title, indicating it is clickable and leads to more content.

<app-card title="Card with Arrow"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="single-action"
  variant="arrow"
  href="#"></app-card>

4. Single Action with Stacked Icon

A single action card with an icon displayed above the content area, useful for visual categorization.

<app-card title="Card with Stacked Icon"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="single-action"
  variant="stacked-icon"
  href="#"></app-card>

5. Single Action with Leading Icon

A single action card with an icon placed to the left of the content, good for compact layouts.

<app-card title="Card with Leading Icon"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="single-action"
  variant="leading-icon"
  href="#"></app-card>

6. Card with Image and Footer

A single action card with a background image at the top and a footer section for additional context.

<app-card title="Card with Image and Footer"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="single-action"
  variant="image"
  href="#"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl1"
  imageAlt="Landscape view"></app-card>

7. Multi Action Default

A multi action card with multiple footer links, each individually clickable. No shadow on desktop.

<app-card title="Multi Action Card"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="multi-action"
  [footerLinks]="multiActionLinks"></app-card>

8. Multi Action with Stacked Icon and Tags

A multi action card with a stacked icon and tag-based footer for categorization.

<app-card title="Card with Tags"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="multi-action"
  variant="stacked-icon"
  [footerTags]="tagList"></app-card>

9. Feature Card (Left Image)

A feature card with the image aligned to the left and content on the right. Responsive: stacks vertically on mobile.

<app-card title="Feature Card Example"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="multi-action"
  variant="image"
  featureAlign="left"
  [footerLinks]="featureCardLinks"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl2"
  imageAlt="Mountain landscape"></app-card>

10. Feature Card (Right Image)

A feature card with the image aligned to the right and content on the left. Responsive: stacks vertically on mobile.

<app-card title="Feature Card Example"
  description="The text used here is card description..."
  type="multi-action"
  variant="image"
  featureAlign="right"
  [footerLinks]="featureCardLinks"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl3"
  imageAlt="Forest landscape"></app-card>
