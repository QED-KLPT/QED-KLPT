Testing Promotional Panels

The following demos showcase the app-promotional-panel component with various types, backgrounds, and configurations following the Queensland Government Design System.

1. Indented Text Panel (Default)

The default promotional panel with a large hero image on the right and content on the left. Best used when a hero image needs greater focus, such as advertising campaign imagery.

<app-promotional-panel [title]="title1"
  [description]="description1"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl1"
  [imageAlt]="imageAlt1"
  [ctaLabel]="ctaLabel1"
  [ctaHref]="'#'"></app-promotional-panel>

2. Indented Image Panel (Larger Text)

A panel with a smaller image container and larger text area. Ideal when there are many dot-points or paragraphs requiring more vertical height, especially for highlighting content without a call-to-action.

<app-promotional-panel [title]="title2"
  [description]="description2"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl2"
  [imageAlt]="imageAlt2"
  [ctaLabel]="ctaLabel2"
  imageAlign="right"></app-promotional-panel>

3. Contained Image Panel (Image Left)

A contained panel with equal visual weight between image and text, image aligned to the left. Works well for smaller content with one primary call-to-action.

<app-promotional-panel [title]="title3"
  [description]="description3"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl3"
  [imageAlt]="imageAlt3"
  [ctaLabel]="ctaLabel3"
  type="contained"
  imageAlign="left"></app-promotional-panel>

4. Contained Image Panel (Image Right)

A contained panel with image aligned to the right. Provides a more distinct visual break from surrounding page content, useful for newsletter signups or secondary CTAs.

<app-promotional-panel [title]="title4"
  [description]="description4"
  [imageUrl]="imageUrl4"
  [imageAlt]="imageAlt4"
  [ctaLabel]="ctaLabel4"
  type="contained"
  imageAlign="right"></app-promotional-panel>

5. Contained Panel (Dark Background)

A contained panel with dark background and white text. Creates strong visual contrast, ideal for important announcements or key messages that need to stand out.

<app-promotional-panel [title]="title5"
