How to use these supports

The practice supports are intentionally broad. They are not written for one specific observable behaviour, but to offer options that educators can adapt using their professional judgement.

The strongest next steps usually come from combining observation evidence, knowledge of the child, and a clear understanding of the learning domain.

Linking from the tool

This page is ready to support deep linking from the Observation Support Tool into the relevant learning domain section when those connections are added.

In the meantime, educators can scroll or use the page headings to move between reflection prompts and domain supports.
