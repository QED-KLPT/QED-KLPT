Analysing and interpreting observational data

Observational data becomes meaningful through a process of analysis and interpretation. Analysis of observational data helps teachers and educators understand what children know and can do: their thinking, knowledge, skills, dispositions and preferences. Analysis goes beyond description; it is a specialised skill drawing on content knowledge, pedagogy knowledge and theoretical perspectives.
