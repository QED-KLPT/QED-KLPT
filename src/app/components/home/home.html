Kindergarten Learning Progressions Toolkit

Practical support for confident early learning

A Queensland Government initiative helping educators observe, reflect, and plan for children's learning across key developmental areas.

Learning areas

Supporting Holistic Development

Explore simple pathways for noticing growth, documenting progress, and supporting each child across connected areas of learning.

L

Language and Literacy

Support vocabulary, phonological awareness, and early reading and writing skills.

E

Executive Function

Develop working memory, attention, cognitive flexibility, and problem-solving skills.

S

Social-Emotional Learning

Foster self-awareness, emotion regulation, empathy, and positive relationships.

P

Physicality

Enhance gross and fine motor skills, coordination, and physical confidence.

M

Mathematics and Numeracy

Build number sense, pattern recognition, spatial reasoning, and mathematical thinking.

C

Child-Centred Assessment

Focus on meaningful observation and assessment without narrowing learning.
