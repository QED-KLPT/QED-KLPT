FOR [Queensland kindergarten teachers and educators]
WHO [want to better understand and support each child’s development]
THE [Kindergarten Learning Progressions Toolkit (KLPT)] is a [voluntary, practical observation and assessment tool]
THAT [turns everyday interactions into meaningful insights for planning and teaching]
UNLIKE [static frameworks, disconnected resources, or one-size-fits-all benchmarking tools]
OUR PRODUCT [provides a consistent, evidence-informed language that enables confident practice and improved learning outcomes.]
