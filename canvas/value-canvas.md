# Value
[Icon] fas fa-gift
## Services
[Icon] fa-solid fa-bell-concierge
### Primary functions
- In-lesson tools for proactive and reactive classroom use
- Exportable data for use in external tools and reports
- Multi-user assessment support
- Resource library searchable by subject, year level, or keyword


## Pain relief
[Icon] fa-solid fa-plus
### How pain is reduced
- Minimal clicks to complete any common task
- Offline mode so poor connectivity never disrupts a lesson
- Pre-built templates for lesson plans, reports, and parent updates
- Mobile-first design — update and check from anywhere in the classroom
- Streamlined onboarding / low training requirements

## Gain creators
[Icon] fa-solid fa-arrow-trend-up
### Exciting new benefits
- Step-by-step lesson planning with trusted scaffolding for new teachers
- In-context help that surfaces exactly when and where it's needed
- Automated progress reporting — low/no manual data entry required
- Parent-facing content supporting teacher effort


# Customer
[Icon] fas fa-user
## Jobs
[Icon] fa-solid fa-list-check
### Tasks I need to do
- Plan and deliver engaging, well-differentiated lessons every day
- Track student understanding with minimal administrative overhead
- Communicate progress clearly to parents, students, and leadership
- Collaborate with and informally support less experienced colleagues
- Manage a complex workload without compromising teaching quality
- Keep professional practice current without constant retraining

## Pain points
[Icon] fa-regular fa-face-frown-open
### Things that frustrate me
- Too many steps to do simple, routine things
- Rigid workflows that can't flex to my classroom context
- Feeling like a data-entry clerk rather than a teacher
- Tools that add to my planning load rather than reducing it
- Slow or unresponsive interfaces — especially during a live lesson
- Overwhelming onboarding with no clear place to start

## Gains
[Icon] fa-regular fa-face-smile
### Benefits I'd welcome
- More time in front of students, less time on admin
- Resources and tools that fit my actual teaching style
- Technology that amplifies my expertise rather than replacing it
- Visible evidence of my students' progress — and my own
- Feeling supported, not surveilled, by the platform and leadership
